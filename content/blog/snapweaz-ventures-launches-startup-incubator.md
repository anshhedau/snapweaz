*A new initiative to support early-stage founders with design, engineering, and strategic capital.*

SnapWeaz Ventures, the in-house arm of our studio focused on co-building with founders, is opening applications for its first incubator cohort. The program is small by design and aimed at founders who have a clear thesis but need a partner to help them go from idea to a fundable product over a focused twelve-week period.

## What the Program Provides

Each founder accepted into the cohort works directly with a senior design and engineering team from SnapWeaz, with the explicit goal of shipping a focused MVP and being ready to raise within the program window. The structure is intentionally short to force decisions and prevent the work from drifting.

## What Founders Get

The package combines studio capacity, strategic capital, and warm introductions where appropriate.

- Twelve weeks of dedicated design and engineering capacity
- A small check to extend the runway through the build
- Weekly working sessions with the SnapWeaz partners
- Introductions to investors who match the thesis
- Lifetime access to the SnapWeaz founder network

## Who We Are Looking For

We are looking for founders working on serious problems in markets they understand from the inside. Industry matters less than depth. A solo founder with five years inside the problem space is a stronger fit than a team chasing a trend they read about last quarter. We are particularly interested in vertical software, infrastructure, and consumer products with clear monetization.

## How to Apply

Applications are open now. The form is short on purpose. We are more interested in clarity of thinking than length of writing. Selected founders will be invited to a working session before any commitment is made on either side. The program kicks off shortly after applications close, and we will only accept a small number of teams to keep the attention high.

## Closing Note

If you are a founder this might be a fit for, head to our contact page and mention the incubator. We will route your note to the right person on our team and respond within a few business days.
