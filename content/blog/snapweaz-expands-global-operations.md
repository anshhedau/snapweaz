*Bringing design and engineering services to Europe and North America to support founders building across borders.*

SnapWeaz is opening up project capacity for clients across Europe and North America, a move that has been quietly in motion for the past year and is now ready to be talked about publicly. The expansion reflects the way our client base has shifted, with more founders running distributed teams and more brands selling into multiple regions from day one.

## Why Now

The past eighteen months have made one thing obvious. The traditional model of working only with local clients no longer matches how product teams are built. Most of the founders we talk to today have engineers in one country, design partners in another, and customers in a third. Working with a studio that can move comfortably across time zones is no longer a luxury, it is a baseline expectation.

## What Changes for Clients

Practically, this means earlier morning availability for European clients, dedicated evening overlap for North American teams, and a billing setup that supports invoicing in the major currencies our clients use.

- Project kickoffs scheduled in the client's preferred time zone
- Dedicated overlap hours for live working sessions
- Invoicing in USD, EUR, GBP, and INR
- Quarterly on-site visits for retainer clients
- Local payment options where possible

## What Stays the Same

Our core principles do not change. Small, senior teams. Focused scope. Honest pricing. Direct contact with the people doing the work. Expansion is about access, not about scaling for its own sake. We have no intention of becoming a large agency, and the team you meet on the first call will still be the team that ships the project.

## How to Engage

If you are a founder or product leader outside of our original markets and have been waiting for the right moment to reach out, this is it. The fastest way to start a conversation is the contact page on our site. We will respond within two business days and, if there is a fit, schedule an introduction call in a time zone that works for you.

## Closing Note

We are looking forward to meeting the founders and teams who will work with us through this next chapter. Thank you to every client, partner, and friend who helped get us here.
