*Recent industry recognition for our work in product design and digital experience.*

SnapWeaz has been recognized this quarter across several industry lists for excellence in product design and digital experience work. We do not chase awards, but we want to acknowledge them when they come, both to thank the teams that selected us and to share what the recognition tells us about the kind of work we should keep doing.

## What the Recognition Is For

The citations focused on a handful of projects from the past year, including a marketplace redesign that tripled conversion rate, an internal tool that cut onboarding time for a B2B client in half, and a brand refresh that quietly repositioned a company from challenger to category leader. Different domains, but a shared approach.

## What These Projects Had in Common

Looking back at the projects that earned the recognition, a few patterns stand out.

- Tight scope, focused on one critical user outcome
- Cross-functional teams that included engineering from day one
- Clear ownership and short decision cycles on the client side
- A research phase that was short but unusually honest
- A launch plan written before the design was finished

## Credit Where It Belongs

Recognition for studio work is always a team result, and that team always includes the client. Every project on the list was made possible by a founder or product leader who trusted the process, made hard cuts to the scope, and protected the team from internal noise. We are grateful for that trust, and we plan to keep earning it.

## What We Are Watching Next

Industry recognition is a lagging indicator. The work that gets celebrated today was usually shipped six to twelve months ago. The more interesting question is what kind of work will be celebrated in 2026. Our bet is on projects that combine careful product design with measurable business outcomes. That is the lens we are using to choose what we take on next.

## Closing Note

Thank you to the judges, peers, and clients who put us on these lists. We will keep working in a way that earns the next ones quietly.
